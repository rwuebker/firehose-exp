Welcome

This web appliction is a project to experiment and provide a glimpse into the power of Twitter's API.

The Twitter firehose blasts appromately 20,000 tweets per second around the globe. Twitter offers a sample (1%) of the steaming firehose for free through the use of their streaming API where user's can track keywords.


Meat Me ASAP

To demonstrate the 'search' API Meat Me ASAP will query previously posted tweets with images and the keyword steak (among other filters in an attempt to keep it relevant to steak).


Twit Roulette

To demonstrate the Firehose Sample API users can enter a search keyword and an amount of time to collect tweets that are streaming at that moment.
